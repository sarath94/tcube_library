                       MAKING A HUNTING BLOW GUN
                              AND DARTS


MATERIALS NEEDED:  4' Long PVC pipe, 1" width
----------------   Pencils (1 for each dart) 
                   Ciggarete buts (clean ones)
                   Piano Wire (10 feet)
                   Glue (Elmers or Super)

MAKING THE GUN
--------------

Ok, just go to your local hardware store, and purchase (steel) 4 feet
of PVC pipe, 1" width, if you want, destroy your enemie's sprinkler
system and get it from there (Scott Carter has a nice sprinkler system
lots of PVC there!)  Anyways, just for aiming purposes, put a little 
notch on the end of the pipe, and glue in a BB, that way you'll have
better aim.  Thats all you need to do for a good gun.

MAKING THE DARTS
----------------

First, get all your pencils together, and remove the metal ends with the
erasers still in them, and be sure not to have any wood inside the metal
whole, the best technique for getting these things off is gripping them 
with a pair of plyers and wiggling the metal piece back and forth.  Ok
now get your piano wire (you can use pins if you want, but piano wire
is much more effective) and insert it into the eraser so it points strait
and just starts to come out the back end in the metal part. Now get your
unused ciggarete butt, tear off the paper so you just have a piece of 
cotton, then put some glue inside the metal so it covers the back end of 
the eraser and stick the cotton inside the whole.  After you stuck in the
butt, tear up the cotton sticking out of the eraser so it sorta flares out
like an afro or something.  Now your dart is made, if you dont understand
exactly how to do this, dont worry, I'll stick in a diagram.

BEST WAYS OF FIRING
-------------------

Aim your blowgun (poison tiped darts if you want) at your favorite target
(Scott Carter makes a good target), put your lips around the pipe (incase
your a dumbfuck, the side opposite of the BB, where the dart is) and blow
as hard as you can, most people try to shoot a burst of air, but this way
is shitty, you dont get as much velocity.
