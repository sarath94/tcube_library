#1-->> "TACK RIFLE"

Materials-->> paper, thick metal pipe with cap on one end, a box of tacks
              and/or roofing nails, lots of gunpowder or equivilant explosive,
              fusing core or solar igniter assembly, and a paper cup.

Procedure--->>  Take the pipe and make sure the cap is firmly on the end.
                Fill the pipe up at around 3-4 in.with lightly packed
                gunpowder.
                Punch a hole in the cup to place the fuse/or gunpowder in.
                Shove the cup with fuse init down the pipe to compress and
                hold the powder. Now that you've done this, fill the remaining
                space in the pipe with tacks, nails, or any metal shrapnel.
                Cover the end with paper and tape it to hold the stull in.
                Make sure the fuse is hanging out of the pipe.
