CARBON TET EXPLOSIVE


A moist  explosive mixture  can be  made from  fine aluminum
powder    combined    with     carbon    tetrachloride    or
tetracloroethylene. This  explosive can be  detonated with a
blasting cap.

MATERIAL REQUIRED
-----------------------------------------------------------

Fine aluminum bronzing powder ( Paint Stores )

Carbon tetrachloride ( Pharmacy, or fire extinguisher fluid)

---- OR ----

Tetracloroethylene ( Dry cleaners, Pharmacy )

Stirring rod ( Wooden spoon )

Mixing container.

Measuring container.

Storage container ( air tight jar )

Blasting cap

Pipe, can or jar.


PROCEDURE:
-------------------------------------------------------------

1) Measure out two parts  aluminum powder to one part carbon
tetrachloride  or  tetrachloroethylene  liquid  into  mixing
container, adding  to powder while stirring  with the wooden
rod.

2) Stir  until the mixture becomes  the consistency of honey
syrup.

3) Store explosive in a jar or similar water proof container
until  ready  to  use.  The  liquid  in  the mixture quickly
evaporates when not confined.

4) Stuff above mixture into a capped pipe section, make sure
its loose,  not to tightly  packed. Insert blasting  cap and
cap open end.
