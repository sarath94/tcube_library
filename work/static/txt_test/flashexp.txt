Flash Explosives

Ingredients :

   * Silver Nitrate (AgNo3) : Should be available at a decent chemist

   * Titanium Powder, or any titanium compound. : Ask at a scrap yard.

   * Water

   * Garden dirt(soil)

Procedure :

   * Mix Silver Nitrate and Titanium Powder and water, and a little garden
     dirt. Leave to dry!
   * Handle with extreme care. This can blow your hands off with ease.
   * Spread across a surface, preferrably by throwing.

WARNING: Any grinding of the mixture will cause a VERY large explosion.
578kJ/g/s (I think that is the power of the explosion)
