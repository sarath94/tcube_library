How to make a Kero Bomb!

ingredients
-----------

75% kerosine

25% pure soap
    ----
fuse

clay

container

how to
--- --

boil kerosine and add soap to it. keep it boiling for a minute or two.
pour kerosine/soap mixture in to a thermos or some sort of container.
get clay and fuse.  put fuse through clay.  shape clay to cover opening of
container.  put clay over container as if to cap it off.  light fuse and
throw!!!!
