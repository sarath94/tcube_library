Pikelo Pete Bomb

Ingredients:

   * 2 Pikelo Pete Fireworks (commonly available around July 4th.)
   * 2-liter plastic bottle.

Procedure:

   * Empty the 2 Pikelo Pete fireworks into the 2-liter bottle.
   * Seal the bottle.
   * Insert a pin hole into the side adjacent to the powder.
   * Insert the wick from the fireworks into the hole.
   * Light the wick and get the hell away!
